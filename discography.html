JORDI 2021


"Jordi" is the seventh studio album by the American pop-rock band Maroon 5. It was released on June 11, 2021, through 222 and Interscope Records. The album is named in honor of the band's late manager, Jordan Feldstein, who passed away in 2017. Overall, "Jordi" represents another chapter in Maroon 5's musical journey, paying homage to their late manager while delivering a collection of radio-friendly tracks that showcase the band's enduring appeal.

RED PILL BLUES 2017



"Red Pill Blues" is the sixth studio album by the American pop-rock band Maroon 5. It was released on November 3, 2017, through 222 and Interscope Records. "Red Pill Blues" was commercially successful, achieving platinum certification in multiple countries. The album's lead single, "Don't Wanna Know," reached the top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, while other singles like "What Lovers Do" and "Wait" also performed well on the charts.

V 2014


"V" is the fifth studio album by the American pop-rock band Maroon 5. It was released on August 29, 2014, through 222 and Interscope Records. Several singles were released from the album, including "Maps," which peaked at number 6 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart in the United States. Other hit singles from the album include "Animals," "Sugar," and "Feelings," all of which achieved commercial success and received extensive radio airplay.

OVEREXPOSED 2012



"Overexposed" is the fourth studio album by the American pop-rock band Maroon 5. It was released on June 20, 2012, through A&M Octone Records. "Overexposed" features a total of 12 tracks on the standard edition and additional tracks on the deluxe edition. Some of the standout tracks on the album include "Payphone" featuring Wiz Khalifa, "One More Night," "Daylight," and "Love Somebody."

SONGS ABOUT JANE 2002


"Songs About Jane" is the debut studio album by the American pop-rock band Maroon 5. It was released on June 25, 2002, through Octone Records and J Records. "Songs About Jane" features a total of 12 tracks on the standard edition and additional tracks on later re-releases and deluxe editions. Some of the standout tracks on the album include "Harder to Breathe," "This Love," "She Will Be Loved," and "Sunday Morning."
